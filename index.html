All Organelles

Note: only eukaryotic cells have organelles.



These are the different types of organelles.


Cell Membrane: Keeps in good things for the cell and kicks things that are bad for the cell out.

Cell Wall: Sturdy Protection only found in plant cells.

Chloropast: Another organelle found in only plant cells, this organelle preforms photosynthesis.

Mitochondria: Powerhouse of the cell. Generates sugars and protiens for the cell.

Vacuole: A storage-like organelle for holding water and nutrients.

Nucleus: Holds the DNA and controls the cell.

Cytoplasm: a jelly-like substance that holds the organelles in place.
